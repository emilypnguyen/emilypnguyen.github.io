About Me


Growing up, I had always wanted to help others; and as an Asian-American it's common for your parents to push the idea of becoming a doctor or nurse. However, I knew that entering the medical field would not bring me happiness as design and art had always brought me the most joy. This thought led me to wanting to study something that would accommodate to both helping people and my creativity.

It is my hope that through my education, I can fulfill my hopes of becoming a user experience (UX) designer. This career would allow me to serve others by creating beneficial and satisfactory products while also challenging my imagination.
